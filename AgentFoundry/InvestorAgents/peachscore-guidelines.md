## Guidelines for Getting First Clients

### 1. Profile Optimization

**Company Profile Setup:**
```
Company Name: Lifetime Oy (DWS IQ Platform)
Tagline: Agentic AI for Intelligent Construction
Description: Real-time edge AI for climate-positive construction.
             Combining NVIDIA Jetson, Google Cloud, and Groq LPU
             to deliver <100ms decisions on construction sites.

Stage: Pre-Seed / Seed
Funding: €150K SAFE target (€3.8M cap, 20% discount)
MRR: €10K (launching Q1 2026)
Employees: 2 founders + 2 contractors
```

**Key Metrics to Add:**
- Target ARR (12 months): €600K
- Pilot customers: 1 (Turner Construction)
- Technology partners: Google, Groq, AWS, GitHub
- Edge devices deployed: 5 (pilot), 50 (target)

### 2. Network Engagement

**Quarterly Events:**
- Attend Peachscore quarterly meetups (Helsinki, Stockholm)
- Schedule coffee meetings via Peachscore introductions
- Connect with construction tech companies in Peachscore network

**Action Items:**
- [ ] Update company profile on Peachscore with DWS IQ launch
- [ ] Attend Peachscore quarterly meetup (next: Q1 2026)
- [ ] Connect with construction tech companies in Peachscore network

### 3. Benchmarking Strategy

**Compare Against 500+ Finnish Scaleups:**
- Industry-specific KPIs (SaaS, AI, Construction Tech)
- Growth rate benchmarks
- Revenue milestones
- Funding stage comparisons

**Key Metrics to Track:**
- MRR growth rate
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Lifetime value (LTV)
- Churn rate
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)

### 4. Investor Introduction Protocol

**Via Peachscore Community Manager:**
1. Request warm introduction through Peachscore platform
2. Provide pilot results as traction proof
3. Highlight credibility stack (Google, AWS, Groq partnerships)
4. Emphasize real customers and partnerships
5. Target Q2 2026 for investor outreach (after pilot success)

### 5. Credibility Stack for Investors

When pitching to VCs, emphasize:

```
✅ Member of Peachscore (Finland's top scaleup community)
✅ Google for Startups ($100K credits) - validated by Google
✅ Groq for Startups ($10K credits) - AI infrastructure partner
✅ AWS for Startups ($25K credits) - edge computing validated
✅ GitHub Enterprise (Lifetime-oy) - professional dev ops
✅ Real customers (Turner Construction)
✅ Real partnerships (ALICE, OpenSpace)
✅ Real infrastructure ($135K in cloud credits)
✅ Real community validation (Peachscore)
```

**Message:** "We're not just a pitch deck. We have real traction, real partnerships, and real community validation."
