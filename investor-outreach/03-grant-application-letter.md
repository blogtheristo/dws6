## Project Overview

### Problem Statement

The EU construction sector faces a critical challenge:
- **2028 deadline** for mandatory embodied carbon reporting
- **3.5 million companies** lack tools for real-time carbon tracking
- **63% of EU emissions** come from SMEs with limited resources
- **Cloud-based solutions** are too expensive and slow for industrial use cases
- **Manual carbon accounting** is error-prone and non-scalable

### Proposed Solution

**DWS IQ 6** is a hybrid edge-cloud platform delivering:

1. **Real-time carbon decisions** via edge AI (<100ms latency)
2. **Agentic automation** for material selection, route optimization, energy management
3. **Digital twins** tracking embodied carbon across entire project lifecycle
4. **EU compliance** built-in for 2028 mandate, Fit for 55, GDPR, ISO 27001
5. **Multi-industry scalability** across 8 high-carbon sectors

### Technical Innovation

**Edge Computing Architecture**
- NVIDIA Jetson for on-site inference
- 25× faster than cloud-only solutions
- 86% cost reduction vs. traditional cloud platforms
- Works offline in remote industrial environments

**Agentic AI System**
- Autonomous decision-making without human intervention
- Learns from real-time operational data
- Optimizes for carbon reduction, cost, and compliance simultaneously
- Integrates with existing construction management tools

**Hybrid Cloud Integration**
- AWS IoT for device management
- Google Cloud for data aggregation
- Groq API for high-speed inference
- Secure EU data residency
